Our Doctrine

We believe:

  • The Trinity of God
  • The Verbal Plenary Inspiration of Scriptures
  • Justification by Faith
  • The Bodily Resurrection of Christ
  • The Blood Atonement of Christ
  • The Personal and Imminent Return of Christ
  • The Virgin Birth of Christ
  • The Person and Work of the Holy Spirit

  • The Priesthood of the Believer
  • The Autonomy of the Church
  • Worldwide Missions
  • Regenerated Church Membership
  • The Personality of Satan
  • The Total Depravity of Natural Man
  • The Reality of Heaven and Hell
  • The Eternal Security of the Believer
  • Separation from the World
  • The Two Ordinances: Baptism and Communion

Greetings,

If you just moved to Columbia, or if you have been living in the Columbia area and are looking for a good church, we would like you to consider Lykesland First Baptist. Please examine our website and you will see why many have reached that conclusion.

To really get to know what our church has to offer your family, you will need to come and visit us! Until then, God bless you!
