How to be saved

  • You must realize that you are a sinner...

    Romans 3:10 "As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one;"
    Romans 3:23 "For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God;" "All" means that everyone has sinned and that everyone has come short of what God requires for entry into heaven.

  • You must realize that the payment for your sin is death...

    Romans 6:23 "For the wages of sin is death..."
    The only thing that will pay the price of sin is blood, therefore, death is the payment. We are doomed to both physical and spiritual death because of our sin. However, There is a way of escape...

  • You must realize that Christ died for you on the cross so that you can escape death, the penalty of sin...

    Romans 6:23 "...but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."
    John 3:16 "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth In him should not perish, but have everlasting life."
    God has given us a way to escape the penalty for our sin.

  • You must accept the gift...

    Romans 10:9 "That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved."
    Romans 10:13 "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved."

    God has promised that if you will accept His free gift, He will save you. If you would like to be saved now, Pray, repenting of your sins, and tell Christ that you realize you are a sinner on your way to Hell.
    Tell Him that you believe that Christ died for your sins and that you want to receive His free gift of salvation. Ask Him to come into your heart and save you.

If you have just accepted Christ as your personal Saviour, please Email us to let us rejoice with you and so we may pray for you.
